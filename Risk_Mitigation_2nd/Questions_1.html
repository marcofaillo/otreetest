First Decision

In this page you will read the instruction regarding your first decision.

Before making the decision you have to answer three control questions.

Instructions

You receive an initial endowment of {{ endowment }} tokens each token has a value of £0.16.

The value of tokens may suffer a loss. In particular, following a random draw, each token may

  • suffer a loss of £0.16 (taking the value of £0) , with a probability of 1/3
  • suffer a loss of £0.08 (taking the value of £0.08) , with a probability of 1/3
  • suffer no loss (remaining £0.16) , with a probability of 1/3

You have to decide how many tokens of the initial endowment you want to insure against the risk of a possible loss resulting from the random draw.

The insurance has a cost of £0.10 per insured token.

Thus, for each token you decide to insure, you will receive £0.06 , while the value of the other tokens will be determined through the random draw.
