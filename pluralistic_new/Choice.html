PHASE 1: Instructions and choice

In phase 1, you will choose one of two options, A or B. Your decision will have an impact on your payoff and on the amount of money that you will donate to a charity. You will be informed of the name of the charity only at the end of the experiment.
At the end of the experiment, the total amount of money given to the charity in this phase will be collected by one of the researchers who will immediately make an online donation which one randomly chosen participant will observe.
The options are the following

  • Option A : You will get 170 CZK . The charity will receive 100 CZK with the probability of 10% and 0 CZK with the probability of 90%.
  • Option B: You will get 100 CZK . The charity will receive 80 CZK with 100% probability.
