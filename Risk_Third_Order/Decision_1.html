FIRST DECISION

First Decision

You receive an initial endowment of {{ endowment }} tokens.

You have to decide how many tokens to invest in an activity with an uncertain return and how many in an activity with a certain return.

In the case of the activity with uncertain return, each token may:

  • take a value of £0.06, with a probability of 2/3
  • take a value of £0.16 with probability of 1/3 ;

In the case of the activity with a certain return, each token takes a value of £0.06.

Once you have made your decision, a random draw will determine the value for the tokens invested in the activity with uncertain return, if any.

 

Click the blue bar to reveal the slider and make your choice.
