THIRD DECISION

Now we ask you to choose between couples of lotteries.

Each lottery has two possible outcome: with a probability p you will get €X and with a probabilit 1-p you will get €Y

For example, lottery A in the first row gives you €2 with a probability of 1/10 and €1.60 with a probability of 9/10; lottery B in the first row gives you €3.85 with a probability of 1/10 and €0.1 with a probability of 9/10. Etc.

In each of the following 10 rows in the table, please select your preferred option between A and B.

When done, please click on the button "Continue".

After you have chosen, one row will be randomly chosen and the lottery you chose will be played.
A random integer number will between 1-10 will be drawn and the outcome of this lottery will be paid to you.

For example, if you chose a lottery giving €2 with a probability of 1/10 and €1,60 with a probability of 9/10. If 1 is drawn, you will be paid €2, conversely, if any other number greater than 1 is drawn, you will be paid €1.60
